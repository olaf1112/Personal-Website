1

Real-time Bus Tracking

RTBT most common features and application advantages.

  • Estimate time of bus arrival (ETA).
  • Identify current location of the bus, plate number, if air-conditioned, and the bus route.
  • Determine bus speed(m/s), and check where the bus is heading.
  • Can view terminals, bus stops and all incoming buses.

For Passengers.

  • Improved Trip Planning.
  • Less guessing and frustration from missed or delayed buses.
  • Builds trust in public transportation by providing timely, consistent information.
  • Know exactly when the next bus will arrive, reducing wait times and uncertainty.

For Transit Agencies

  • Better Fleet Management - Monitor buses in real time to optimize routes, reduce delays, and reroute in emergencies.
  • LData Collection and Analysis - Helps analyze ridership patterns, peak times, and bottlenecks for future planning.
  • Builds trust in public transportation by providing timely, consistent information.
  • Reduced Operational Costs - Efficient dispatching and fewer complaints can lead to cost savings.

Interchanging Dmmmsu Knowledge

University community discussion forum named “IDK” as both an acronym for “Interchanging DMMMSU Knowledge” and a nod to the informal “I don’t know”.

  • A space to ask questions about assignments, exams, or course material.
  • Faculty can share resources and announcements.
  • Connects students from different campuses, departments, and year levels.
  • Builds a stronger sense of belonging and student identity.
  • Fast, easy way to discuss campus issues, events, schedules, or opportunities.
  • Reduces reliance on slow or one-way communication like email or bulletin boards.
  • Available 24/7, unlike in-person office hours or consultations.

Charity Management System

A Charity Management System (CMS) is a digital platform designed to help nonprofit organizations, charities, or student-led causes organize, manage, and track their activities, donations, volunteers, and outreach efforts.

  • Combines all charity operations (donations, events, volunteers) in one platform.
  • Ensures transparency and builds donor trust.
  • Tracks volunteer hours and participation history.
  • Detailed reports show where funds go and how resources are used.
  • Allows people to donate online.
4

Library Management System (LMS)

Designed to manage and streamline the day-to-day operations of a library, whether physical or digital. It helps track books, manage members, process loans and returns, and organize catalogues efficiently.

  • Tracks availability, location, and status of books (issued, returned, reserved).
  • Reduces chances of book misplacement or duplication.
  • Automates the borrowing and return process, saving time for librarians and students.
  • Users can search and reserve books online anytime.
  • Encourages more usage of library resources.
